About Us

We are a network of professionals at your disposal to make parenting a better journey, less lonely and exhausting, a sensitive, empathetic, and more comfortable path. We are the extra help you need to parent successfully and effectively. You will become the well-informed mother your child will enjoy, having access to a network of highly qualified professionals, top in their fields, ready to advise you whenever you need them.
A beautiful African proverb says, "It takes a village to raise a child."
And it's true, it takes people committed to your child's well-being and yours too; it takes professionals, it takes a friend. We want to be part of your village, to be that friend who helps you manage whatever you need.
If you need a nanny, we have various professionals in our team. That's our specialty; they excel, are qualified, and will not only care for your child but also support their skill and potential development.
Our team includes psychologists, occupational therapists, psycho-pedagogues, nannies, and even party planners and decorators. Whatever you need for your child!
We also have a team of therapists, sexologists, masseuses, personal trainers, and nutritionists.
We help you nurture!

We have all the tools for your peace of mind
