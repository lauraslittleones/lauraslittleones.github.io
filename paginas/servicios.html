Available Services

Professional Baby Sitters at home, available day and night
  • Two types of services: occasional and regular
  • Tutoring: Educational support sessions for leveling, developing study habits, encouraging self-awareness of predominant learning styles, and structured sessions to achieve goals set with parents and/or educators. These sessions are led by educators from various levels and fields: preschool, elementary, English, or math teachers.
  • Play Groups: Experiences for parents and babies. Stimulation guided by our professionals.
  • Group workshops with various themes to develop creativity, motor skills, and social abilities
  • Parenting Advice: Provided by psychologists either at home or online.
  • Occupational Therapy at home
  • Psychological Therapy
  • Mom Groups: To vent, listen, and learn from each other

We have all the tools for your peace of mind
